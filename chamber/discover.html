Discover Ejido Santa Fe Chamber of Commerce

Demographics

Ejido de Santa Fe has a population of 539 residents with 7.98% of the inhabitants identifying as indigenous and 3.71% speaking indigenous "Cora" language.

Attractions

While Ejido Santa Fe is a small community, the broader municipality of Rosamoraada offers several points of interest:

  • Parish of the Immaculate Conception Mary: A historic church located int hte municipal seat, showcasing colonial architecture.
  • former Hacienda of Chilapa: An old estate reflecting the region's history and cultural heritage.
  • Archaelogical Remains at the Municipal Presidency Artifacts and structures that provide insights into the area's pre-Hispanic civilizations.

Events and Festivals

The municipality hosts various cultural and religious events throughout the year.

  • Traditional Fest of the Immaculate Conception: An annual celebration featuring religious ceremonies, music, and local cuisine
  • Navy Day (June 1):A military parade commemorating the armed forces, with festivities in coastal towns like San Blas.
